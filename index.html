Is Magic Grab free?

Yes! We utilize open-source segregation models and the Gemini AI free tier to keep professional tools accessible to everyone.

How precise is the extraction?

Our vision engine handles complex edges and textures with 99% precision, isolating subjects perfectly from their backgrounds.

Are my images kept private?

Absolutely. Your images are processed for layer segregation and never stored on our servers. Your data stays in your workspace.

Can I use it on my phone?

Yes! Magic Grab is fully responsive and optimized for creative work on both desktop and mobile browsers.
